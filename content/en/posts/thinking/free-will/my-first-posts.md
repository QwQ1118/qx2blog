<p style="text-align:center; font-size:1.2rem; font-weight:bold; margin-bottom:2rem;">Dedicated to me, who successfully built a personal blog(80%) at 5 a.m.</p>

In fact, for a long time, I have been a particularly floating person, I often can't "settle", sometimes I even feel that I am standing on some kind of edge, maybe life, maybe life, and I will also think about what I use to keep me.

Writing a book? But I'm not a big person.
Chasing fame and fortune? But I have not been able to do anything contrary to my morals and thoughts for a long time.

So what to do?

Due to some kind of coincidence, on the day Michelle Yeoh won the award for the instantaneous universe, I kept looking for resources on the Internet, and the moment I finished watching the movie, I suddenly found that I seemed to have found a way to keep it.

That's right, build a personal website.

From 2023, I started to complete my ideas, but because I didn't know much about this field at that time, and I needed to get my graduation thesis, I can say without shame that I only completed the registration on GitHub at that time, and then turned to 2024, after I went to Fujian with my parents after the Spring Festival, I went to work in Xi'an, which was probably the most painful experience of my life so far.

At the end of 2024, I woke up from my mediocre life, and this sentence kept swirling in my head about what I wanted to leave behind.

So in 2025, I started to seriously learn python, web, LLM, and everything else I thought I needed to learn, and although I can admit that I didn't learn to the point of being superior, I am at least no longer blank now. I tried to build a website at the beginning of the year, and it paid off (pictured), but later because of the pain in real life, I put this project on hold again.

![25第一版网站](/img/freewill_01.jpg)

Fortunately, in 2025.09, I got out of the environment that made me miserable and returned to my hometown, Kunming.

After constantly cleaning up my fat body and worn out spirit due to work, on May 1st, I suddenly felt that my three souls and seven souls seemed to have all returned, so I turned on the computer and picked up my idea of building a website again, and luckily, I succeeded 80% today, which is something I can't imagine, because no one taught me, I only had deepseek and my thin basic knowledge.

Anyway, by the time I pushed it all to github and cloudflare, a part of me was already everywhere.
